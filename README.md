## ⭐ Features

* **Live Group Syncing:** Register an account, then create or join a secure group with your clanmates. Whenever anyone in your group starts, clears, or updates a timer, it instantly synchronizes across everyone's screen in real-time.

* **Ghost Mode (Click-Through):** Press `Alt+Shift+P` on your keyboard from anywhere to toggle Ghost Mode. When active, the overlay locks in place, drops its background, and completely ignores mouse clicks so it never interferes with your gameplay.

* **Global Server Presets:** Access a built-in, server-synced list of common Pax Dei timers, beautifully organized into collapsible category folders (e.g., Iron, Steel, PvP).

* **Custom Timers & Local Presets:** Create your own on-the-fly timers with custom names, custom durations, and optional image URLs. You can even save them as Local Presets so you can re-use them with a single click later!

* **Adjustable Overlay Opacity:** Use the opacity slider in the settings menu to make the overlay transparent so it blends perfectly into your HUD.

* **Clean UI Filtering:** Uncheck global timers you don't care about to hide them from your list and keep your menu compact.

* **Auto-Updates:** The installer version of the application automatically detects, downloads, and applies updates in the background.
